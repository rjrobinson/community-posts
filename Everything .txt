<p>In this blog, I am sharing my learning that are gained from the Week-2 of AZ-104: Microsoft <code>Azure Administrator Bootcamp</code>.</p>

<p>This boot camp is really useful for those who want to pass the AZ-104 exam. It was a great opportunity to participate in the Microsoft Azure AZ-104 Bootcamp hosted by the <code>Azure Developer Community</code> in collaboration with <code>Whizlabs</code> and <code>Reskill</code>.</p>

<p>The Microsoft Azure AZ-104 certification is an administrator-level certification that measures our ability to perform the following technical tasks:</p>

<ul>
<li>Manage Azure IDs and governance. </li>
<li>Implement and manage storage. </li>
<li>Deploy and manage Azure compute resources. </li>
<li>Configure and manage virtual networks. </li>
<li>Monitor and protect Azure resources. </li>
</ul>
