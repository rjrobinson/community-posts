<p>The benefits of continuous delivery are enormous, and almost <strong>every</strong> engineer you ask would agree. But almost no organizations are shipping software in under 15 minutes. So what’s stopping us?</p>

<p>Continuous Delivery, the action of having a short feedback loop when shipping software to production, is the best way for engineering teams to operate. In this incredible episode of <a href="https://devinterrupted.com/podcast/no-continuous-delivery-then-walk-out/">Dev Interrupted</a>, Charity Majors, CTO of Honeycomb.io, explains how to implement continuous delivery, and why you should walk out if your organization doesn’t support the practice.</p>
